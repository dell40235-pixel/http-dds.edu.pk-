About Our Institute

Die deutsche Sprachschule has been teaching German as a foreign language since 2020. Founded by Muhammad Arsalan Afzal, our institute provides quality German language education with experienced instructors and proven teaching methods.

We offer courses for all levels from A1 to B1 with flexible online and physical class options.

  • Certified instructors
  • Small class sizes
  • Interactive learning
  • Cultural immersion
  • Flexible timings
